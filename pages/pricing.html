FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

What does the price include?▾
All plans include: domain registration (.in or .com), 1 year hosting, SSL certificate, mobile-responsive design, and post-launch support (duration varies by plan). There are no hidden charges.
Can I pay in installments?▾
Yes! We offer a 50-50 payment plan: 50% at project start and 50% after you're satisfied with the final result. For Pro plans, a 3-installment option is also available.
What happens after the 1-year hosting expires?▾
Hosting renewal is approximately ₹2,000–₹4,000/year depending on your store's size and traffic. We'll remind you 60 days before expiry. You can also switch to your own hosting — we'll help with migration.
Do I get source code / ownership?▾
100% yes. You own your website completely. We hand over all source files, login credentials, and access. You're never locked in with us.
Can I upgrade my plan later?▾
Absolutely! You only pay the difference. If you started with Starter (₹4,999) and want to upgrade to Growth (₹9,999), you pay ₹5,000. We'll add the features to your existing store.
