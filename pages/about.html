Our Mission

Local Shops Deserve the Same Digital Tools as Big Brands

India has over 63 million small businesses. Most are offline. Meanwhile, e-commerce giants are capturing their customers. We're changing that.

ShopOnline was founded in 2022 with a simple belief: every local shop owner deserves an affordable, beautiful online presence — without needing to learn tech.

We handle everything — from domain to launch — so you can focus on what you do best: running your shop and serving your customers.
